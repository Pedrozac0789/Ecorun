Here you will find some places where you can take a beautiful walk, enjoying a sunset, such as the Bebedouro Lagoon, in addition to observing a splendid fauna and flora of our beloved city and taking the kids to play in playgrounds and even have a snack afterwards. Meet these places below!

Places to do outdoor physical activity in Parnaíba

Quadrilhodromo

The Quadrilhodromo is a space that goes beyond June festivities, being a versatile place to practice physical activities. Surrounded by the energy of traditional dances, this space becomes a lively stage for varied exercises. Between the typical choreographies and cultural celebrations, there are opportunities for walks, runs or even outdoor dancing classes, providing a vibrant and relaxing atmosphere to take care of your health while connecting with local tradition, transforming the act of exercising into a fun and culturally enriching experience.


Information:


Time:4pm to 6pm;
Mileage:1.8km three complete laps;
Walking time: 16 minutes;

Avenida Três de Maio

Três de Maio Avenue is more than a busy road. With its wide sidewalks and vibrant urban landscape, it becomes an invitation for outdoor physical activities. Its inviting spaces are ideal for invigorating walks, dynamic runs or even cycling, giving residents and visitors the opportunity to exercise while enjoying the city's energy. Between the coming and going of people and the rhythm of the avenue, it becomes a multifaceted setting for taking care of your health and staying active, transforming your exercise routine into an urban experience full of vitality.


Information:


Time: 5:40am to 7am;
4:30pm to 7pm;
Mileage: 4.4 km round trip;
Walking time:1 hour;

Bebedouro Lagoon

Bebedouro' s lagoon with its serene landscape invites you to exercise in nature. Its tree-lined banks and surrounding trails are an ideal setting for invigorating walks, revitalizing runnings and even peaceful yoga sessions. The combination of natural beauty with the opportunity to exercise outdoors makes this lagoon a perfect destination for taking care of physical and mental health, allowing visitors to reconnect with themselves while enjoying the serenity of the environment.

Airport

Parnaíba Airport, although known as a departure and arrival point, can be a surprising space to practice physical exercise. Its wide spaces, lanes and open areas offer opportunities for revitalizing walks, energizing runs or even stretching and own yoga sessions, providing an unusual alternative to physical activity. While planes taking off and landing, visitors can enjoy a dynamic health-care environment, transforming the airport parking into an unexpected and functional place to exercise.


Information:


Time: 4pm to 6pm;
Mileage:3,4km one complete laps;
Walking time: 50 minutes;

Av São Sebastião (Love Square to Want Bread 2)

Amor Square is a charming place that combines the romanticism of the name with the opportunity to take care of your health through exercise. With its peaceful and cozy atmosphere, the square becomes an ideal space for invigorating walks, lively runs or even stretching and relaxing exercises. Between flower benches and leafy trees, visitors have the chance not only to exercise, but also to appreciate the natural beauty, transforming body care into moments of serenity and connection with nature.
